CHARACTER CREATION HELP
═══════════════════════

STEP 1: ROLL CHARACTERISTICS
─────────────────────────────
Press 'r' to roll all 7 characteristics.
Each is rolled as 2d6 × 8 (range: 16-96).

CHARACTERISTICS:
• STR (Strength): Affects damage dealt
• SPD (Speed): Affects initiative
• STA (Stamina): Endurance
• CRG (Courage): Affects initiative
• LCK (Luck): Affects to-hit chance
• CHM (Charm): Social interactions
• ATT (Attraction): Personal appeal

Color coding:
  Green: High values (good!)
  Yellow: Medium values
  Red: Low values (consider re-rolling)

STEP 2: SELECT EQUIPMENT
─────────────────────────
Choose your starting weapon:
• Short Sword: Balanced (+5 damage)
• Axe: Powerful (+8 damage)

STEP 3: REVIEW CHARACTER
─────────────────────────
Confirm your character details.
Life Points (LP) = sum of all characteristics.
Skill (SKL) starts at 0.
Power (POW) is locked until unlocked in game.

Press Enter to finalize and start your adventure!

Press Esc or ? to close
