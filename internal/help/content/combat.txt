COMBAT HELP
═══════════

COMBAT ACTIONS
──────────────
Each turn, choose one action:

ATTACK
  Roll to hit enemy.
  • Need 7+ on 2d6
  • Modified by Luck and Skill
  • On hit, roll damage (1d6 × 5 + bonuses)

CAST SPELL
  Open magic screen to cast spells.
  Combat spells: FIREBALL, POISON NEEDLE, ARMOUR, XENOPHOBIA
  Exit spells: INVISIBILITY, PARALYSIS

USE ITEM
  Activate special items like Healing Stone.

FLEE
  Attempt to escape combat.
  May fail based on circumstances.

COMBAT FLOW
───────────
1. Initiative determines turn order
   Your Init = 2d6 + SPD + CRG + LCK
   
2. Higher initiative acts first

3. Take turns until one side reaches 0 LP

DAMAGE & DEFENSE
────────────────
Your damage:
  (1d6 × 5) + (STR/16) + Weapon - Enemy armor

Enemy damage:
  Enemy base damage - Your armor - Your shield

Active spell effects:
• ARMOUR: -10 incoming damage
• XENOPHOBIA: -5 enemy damage

DEATH SAVES
───────────
When LP reaches 0:
• One death save per combat (2d6, need 7+)
• Success: LP = 1, continue fighting
• Failure: Character dies

TIPS
────
• Cast ARMOUR before tough fights
• Use FIREBALL on high-LP enemies
• Save Healing Stone for emergencies
• Equipment locked during combat

Press Esc or ? to close
